Daily mobility

Discover how people make their daily trip to the supermarket and how this relates to infrastructure and the surrounding environment.

Fieldwork (300 points)

Find an Albert Heijn (grocery store) outside the city center. Observe the surrounding environment:

A.

Is there residential/housing available nearby?
Little / medium / a lot

B.

Are there other services or amenities nearby?
Little / medium / a lot

C.

Is there a transit stop nearby?
Yes / no / can’t see one

D.

Is there a bike path nearby?
Yes / no / can’t see one



E.

Is there an underground car parking garage available?
Yes / no


F.

Count how many parking places are available for:
a. cars
b. bicycles
c. scooters

G.

Observe 10 customers arriving or departing the store. How many are
a. by foot?
b. by bike?
c. by scooter?
d. by car?
e. by transit?

H.

Did they come by bike, foot, car or public transport?


I.
