Traffic flow

Central Station/Understand how modal transitions work and how traffic flows in public places.

Observation (150 points)

At Central Station, find the bike tunnel leading to the ferries.

A.

What is painted on the walls of the tunnel?



B.

North of the bike tunnel, near the ferries, you find a large area of “shared space”. Observe behavior for 5-10 minutes. Write down at least 5 “rules” for how people navigate the space.

☆ Bonus (200 points)

Obstruct the traffic flow by dropping something, walking aimlessly, etc. while your partner observes.

C.

What happens to the flow?


D.

Show us the most outrageous thing you saw in the Central Station environment (with a photo as proof).

Fieldwork (100 points)

At the shared space area, gather social data about the cyclists you see: gender difference, children present, talking to someone, holding hands, carrying something, smiling, etc. Choose 3 different variables.

E.

How many out of 25 bicyclists for each factor do you observe?
